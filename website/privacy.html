Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how Autoposter AI manages, protects, and uses the data collected from our users. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data integrity and transparency.

1. Data Collection Transparency

Autoposter AI collects only the information strictly necessary for the execution of automated social media publishing. This includes:

  • Authentication Data: We use official OAuth 2.0 flows to obtain temporary access tokens. Autoposter AI never sees or stores your account password.
  • Profile Metadata: We retrieve your public username and display name to identify your channel within the dashboard.
  • Content Media: The video files and text captions you voluntarily upload to the Autoposter AI environment for processing.

2. Technical Data Handling

Data processing within Autoposter AI follows strict technical protocols:

  • Temporary Processing: Media files are stored temporarily during the re-encoding and AI analysis phase and are automatically purged after successful publication.
  • AI Analysis: Content is analyzed using the Google Gemini AI API to generate metadata. This data is handled in accordance with Google's Enterprise Privacy standards.
  • Encryption: All data in transit between Autoposter AI and Social Media APIs is protected via SSL/TLS 1.3 encryption.

3. Third-Party Platform Compliance

Autoposter AI integrates with third-party platforms. By using our service, you agree to their respective privacy terms:

  • TikTok: Data is handled according to the TikTok Privacy Policy.
  • Google/YouTube: Our YouTube integration uses YouTube API Services. You agree to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Meta: Instagram operations comply with the Instagram Data Policy.

4. User Rights and Data Control

You have full control over your data within Autoposter AI:

  • Access Revocation: You can revoke Autoposter AI's access at any time through your social media account's security dashboard.
  • Data Deletion: You may request the deletion of any tokens associated with your instance of Autoposter AI.

5. Changes to This Policy

Autoposter AI reserves the right to update this policy to reflect changes in our app functionality or legal requirements. Users will be notified of any significant changes via the application dashboard.
